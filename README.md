# ALMA Core

> Helping people understand themselves through cognitive reflection.

---

## What is ALMA?

ALMA Core is an open research project exploring how artificial intelligence can help people better understand themselves, their relationships, and the psychological patterns that shape their lives.

Unlike traditional AI assistants, ALMA does not focus on giving quick advice.

Its goal is to build a continuously evolving understanding of a person, identify recurring relationship patterns, generate hypotheses, and support long-term self-reflection.

---

## Core Principles

- Understand before advising.
- Search for causes, not symptoms.
- Every conversation improves the model.
- Every conclusion remains open to revision.
- Psychological clarity is more valuable than immediate answers.

---

## Vision

ALMA is not a chatbot.

ALMA is a cognitive engine designed to investigate human relationship dynamics and build a living psychological model over time.

---

## Current Status

The project is currently focused on designing the cognitive architecture that will power future implementations.

Development has not yet begun.
