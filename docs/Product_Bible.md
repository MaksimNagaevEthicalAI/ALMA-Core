Every strategic product decision should remain consistent with this document.

# PB-001 — Problem Statement

## The Problem

People often repeat the same relationship patterns without understanding why.

Most individuals recognize the consequences of these patterns only after emotional pain, conflict, or separation has already occurred.

Existing solutions address only parts of the problem.

Dating platforms focus on finding partners rather than understanding compatibility.

Therapy often begins after significant psychological distress and is not designed to accompany millions of people through everyday relationship decisions.

General-purpose AI assistants provide useful conversations but do not build a persistent psychological understanding of the individual across time.

As a result, people repeatedly ask the same questions:

- Why do I keep choosing similar partners?
- Why can't I let go after a breakup?
- Why do healthy relationships sometimes feel emotionally unfamiliar?
- Why do I continue repeating patterns that I already recognize?

These questions are rarely isolated events.

They are symptoms of deeper psychological mechanisms that remain largely invisible to the individual.

ALMA exists to help people understand the hidden psychologigal mechanisms that shape their relationships.

## Design Philosophy

ALMA is designed to increase understanding before attempting to influence decisions.

Insight comes before advice.

# PB-002 — Mission

## Mission

ALMA exists to help people better understand themselves.

Human relationships are one of the most powerful mirrors of our inner world. They reveal our values, fears, emotional patterns, expectations, and unconscious ways of connecting with others.

By combining long-term reflection with cognitive modeling, ALMA helps people recognize the hidden psychological mechanisms that shape their choices, relationships, and personal growth.

Its purpose is not to make decisions for people, but to help them make better decisions through deeper self-understanding.
