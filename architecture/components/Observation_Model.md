# Observation Model

Version: 0.1

Status: Draft

---

## Purpose

The Observation Model defines how ALMA transforms conversations into structured observations.

Observations are the smallest meaningful units of psychological evidence.

Unlike raw conversation history, observations represent interpreted facts that can later support patterns and hypotheses.

---

## Fundamental Principle

ALMA never reasons directly from conversations.

It reasons from observations extracted from conversations.

Conversations are temporary.

Observations become evidence.
